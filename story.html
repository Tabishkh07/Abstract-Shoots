Since childhood, XYZ has been inclined towards creativity. His journey toward success began with Brand Communications at a Mumbai-based firm, where he joined the Indian corporate sector with the initial responsibility of rebranding a nationwide listed company. XYZ applied his expertise in visual design to not only enhance the image of the Group but also do so in a cost-effective and efficient manner. His efforts impressed the top management, and at a very young age, he was prematurely double promoted to the position of Manager - MarCom.

He moved on the Corporate ladder to help several brands to either rebrand or establish new brand identities.

Later, he developed a strong interest in Commercials and Production Design and considers himself fortunate to have worked in the Indian film industry—the largest source of mass content creation in India. He began contributing to concept development, design, and cinematic art décor, including set design for films, television, and OTT platforms. During this period, he not only helped rebrand several companies but also worked on large-scale set designs and high-profile events.

Eventually, he combined all his creative experience and content creation knowledge to launch his own brand—Abstract Shoots. Abstract Shoots delivers tailor-made, high-quality video, photo, and branding solutions to a diverse clientele. Its clients range from individuals and small businesses to large multinational corporations. Over the years, Abstract Shoots has established itself as a turnkey expert in producing premium video, photo, and branded content.
