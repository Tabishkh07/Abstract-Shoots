Since his childhood, Mubashshir was inclined towards creativity. The journey of success began with Brand Communications in a Mumbai-based firm. During his tenure, he was responsible for efficient, satisfactory, and economical operation in the area of marketing & Corporate Communications for the company. Not only was it a successful Branding across India but it won the hearts of the top management and he was pre-maturely double promoted as Manager – MarCom. During his time in Corporate, he gave several Brands a new identity.

After this, he developed an inclination towards Commercials and Production Designing and considers himself fortunate enough to work in Bollywood (Indian Film Industry) – the biggest source of mass content creation in India. He started to work in Concept, Design, and Décor of Cinematic Art and Set Designing for Films/TV/OTT Platforms. During his tenure in Bollywood, he gave new Brand identities to three Brands and also worked on Large-scale Events.

After that, he moved on to club all his creative experience and knowledge about Content creation in order to launch his own Brand which is Abstract Shoots. Abstract Shoots delivers tailormade high-quality video, photo, and branding solutions to its esteemed clients. The clients come from every walk of life, be it Corporate or individual and Abstract Shoots has become a turnkey expert in creating high-quality video, photo, and branding content.
